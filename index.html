How to Use Zap Text Cleaner

Using our tool is simple and instantaneous. Follow these steps to sanitize your text:

  1. Paste: Copy your messy text from any source (Word, PDF, Webpage, Email) and paste it into the input box above.
  2. Clean: Click the processing button to instantly strip away fonts, colors, weights, and hidden coding blocks.
  3. Copy: Click the copy button to send your fresh, clean plain text straight to your clipboard.

Why Use a Online Text Formatting Stripper?

When you copy text from applications like Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or digital PDFs, the text carries hidden rich-text formatting codes. When you paste that text into content management systems, emails, or code editors, those hidden styles can break your design, alter layouts, or cause unexpected bugs.

Zap Text Cleaner acts as a total digital reset. It intercepts your copy-pasted layout and strips it down to standard UTF-8 plain text. It is the fastest way to format text online without annoying downloads or complex settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my text safe and private? Yes. Our plain text converter operates entirely within your web browser. Your text is processed locally on your machine and is never uploaded, stored, or sent to any external servers. Your data remains completely private.
